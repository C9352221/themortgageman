About Me

I was born and raised in Sarasota and graduated from Cardinal Mooney before earning my business degree from the University of South Florida. From there I began my career in the mortgage industry with one driving passion: helping people achieve homeownership and financial success. Over 25 years I have grown into a top producer at my company and across the state, recognized for dedication, consistency, and a client-focused approach.

What started as a career quickly became a calling. In the business of helping people navigate one of the most important financial decisions of their lives, time has flown by. I take pride in building lasting relationships, delivering exceptional service, and guiding clients with honesty, professionalism, and expertise every step of the way.

My mission is simple: to serve as a trusted advisor, simplifying home financing by exploring every option and providing personalized, stress-free lending experiences. Business is always good when you're helping people!
